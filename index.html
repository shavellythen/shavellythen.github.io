A little bit about me...I am often times confused with an extrovert because I am friendly, outspoken and a leader. However, I conside my self an introvert because social outing drain my energy. I searched google to find the answer for this puzzle and it says that there is a hybrid personality trait. An ambivert is someone who exhibits qualities of both introversion and extroversion, and can flip into either depending on their mood, context, and goals.

Ambiverts have also been called:

  • Outgoing introverts: An introvert who can be outgoing in certain situations, around certain people, or when they absolutely need to.
  • Antisocial extroverts: An extrovert who needs time to recharge before socializing, or who likes to be alone more than a typical extrovert.
  • Social introverts: An introvert who can dial up into extroversion when needed.

I think that I am an antisocial extrovert. I don't like to turn on my camera on any zoom meeting, I don't like to go out a lot or be around people for a long period of time. However, I know how to be social and I am not a shy person. I find this very interesting.
